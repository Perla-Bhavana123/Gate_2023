INTERNATIONAL CENTRES


Centres at Dhaka (Bangladesh), Kathmandu (Nepal), Kuala Lumpur (Malaysia), Male (Maldives), Port Louis (Mauritius), Singapore (Singapore) and Thimphu (Bhutan) are withdrawn due to operational issues. GATE 2023 Examination will NOT be conducted in these cities.


In Dubai (UAE) the examination on 5th February (for papers BM, CY, EC, EE, ES, XH) and 12th February 2023 (for papers CE1, CE2, MN and ST) will NOT be conducted.


Candidates registered for examination in these cities can exercise one of the following options:

You can withdraw your GATE 2023 candidature and get the full refund for application fees. You can select one of the cities available in India (See gate.iitk.ac.in). You will be refunded the original registration fees. However, to write examination in India, you have to pay the fees for PER PAPER as follows:

Candidate must login to their account to exercise Option B. You must convey their decision by sending email to gate@iitk.ac.in by 5th December 2022 with the following details:
1) Your enrolment id and name
2) Option Chosen - A) Withdrawal of GATE 2023 Candidature OR
         B) Writing Examination in India
